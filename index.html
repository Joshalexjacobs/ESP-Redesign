About Us

A fixture in the heart of Providence since 1997, East Side Pockets serves its customers consistently great Middle Eastern food seven days a week! From 10am to late night Monday through Saturday, and until 10pm on Sunday.

Falafel (absolutely the best around!), gyros, wraps, baklava, grape leaves, salads, hummus, and tzatziki are available along with all items on our menu for take-out or sit-down dining. Lines move quickly during lunch and dinner hours, and the portions and quality are well worth the short wait.
